Catalog highlights — 2026-05-24

  • URL normalizer bug fix: sampled HEAD audit caught a normalizeUrl() bug that lowercased entire URLs including path/query (case-sensitive on most servers). Fix preserves case in path/query/fragment. Refresh sweep restored 14,331 URLs (26% of catalog) to correct case — worst hits vscode-extensions 99.6%, HF models 66%, HF datasets 58%. Full story: 14,331 URLs Restored.
  • Two new crawler noise filters: year-prefix section-headers (e.g. "2021 Result", "2023 Competition") and bare github.com self-section pointers (e.g. github.com/foo/awesome-bar#books where the entry name alnum-matches the anchor). Recent scrubs removed 3 records from vector-db-and-rag and 27 from self-hosted-software; zero false positives across the other 18 datasets. Verifiable buyer-side via noise-audit jq snippets. Methodology: FAQ Q26, api.html#data-quality.
  • New endpoint /cross-dataset-overlap.json: lists every URL that appears in 3+ of the 20 catalog datasets (currently 208). Cross-leverage signal for buyer-agents doing market-mapping; bundle-justification reference for the buyer's guide. Documented in OpenAPI 1.3.0 schema CrossDatasetOverlap. Worked jq snippets in examples #cross-leverage.
  • Two stale hand-floors corrected: llmops-and-eval 490+ → 500+ (actual 573), vector-db-and-rag 190+ → 200+ (actual 224). self-hosted-software 2,300+ → 2,200+ (post-noise-scrub 2,294 dropped below floor).
  • Free sample expansion 20 → 30 records: every dataset's free JSON + CSV sample now ships 30 diverse records (round-robin from categories) instead of 20. ~50% more coverage of each dataset's field shape, still well below the full-purchase ZIP size.
  • Cross-leverage surfaces: 208 tools span 3+ of the 20 datasets — see /cross-leverage.html for the human-readable top-40 table, /cross-dataset-overlap.json for the full programmatic list, and the #cross-niche section on every per-dataset detail page for the dataset-specific subset.
  • Storefront cleanup: the "Complete Developer Data Bundle" SKU (all-20 mega-bundle) was sold_out on Gumroad — removed from the site's nav CTA, bundle list, and per-detail JSON-LD isPartOf until the SKU returns. Storefront homepage CTA points at jhonnyronnie.gumroad.com for the time being. The 3 themed bundles (Dev Stack / Platform Builder / ML Builder) remain available.
